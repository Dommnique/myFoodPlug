What is our story?

Welcome to Myfoodplug! Myfoodplug is a food delivery business that came to life, due to how passionate we are about bringing to the public home-made meals at budget-friendly rates, putting the state of the present economy at heart. We are fully based in Abuja, Nigeria and we are looking forward to spreading across the different states in Nigeria with time. We deliver our delicious meals across any location within Abuja. One of the numerous things that we are known for, is the unforgettable taste our meals would leave in your mouth. We understand how to create burst of flavors in whatever meal that we are cooking and the health benefits of most ingredients and condiments that are mostly left out. We incorporate them into our meals for the complete experience. In the sense that, not only do we serve delicious meals, we serve nourishing ones too. Our goal is give you top-notch service and Fast door-step delivery in any quantity, on the days that you are not opportuned to cook for any reason. Myfoodplug is the only food plug for you
